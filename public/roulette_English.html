What is Roulette?

A casino game where players throw balls into a rotating disk and guess where they will fall.
Roulette, also known as the queen of casinos, is offered in many casinos.

basic rule

summary

The wheel (turntable) has evenly divided pockets into which the balls fall and is marked with numbers.
The pockets are colored red or black, with 0 (00, 000) being green.
The two main types of wheels are European style and American style. Other types include Mexican style (39 categories) and French style (37 categories, the same as European style, but with different table layouts).
The sequence of numbers and odds vary depending on the type of wheel.

  • European Style 37 categories (1 to 36, 0)
  • American Style 38 categories (1 to 36, 0, 00)
  • Mexican style 39 categories (1 to 36, 0, 00, 000)
As for the American style, it is possible to see wheels with different number sequences in different regions.

Game Procedures

  1. The dealer rings the bell once to inform the players that betting has begun.
  2. Players place their bets individually.
  3. The dealer twists the top knob to turn the wheel and throws the ball in the opposite direction of rotation.
  4. The player makes an additional bet. Or, the player changes his or her bet.
  5. The dealer declares the end of betting while a certain amount of time has elapsed and the ball is spinning.
    The dealer declares the end of betting while a certain amount of time has elapsed and the ball is spinning.
  6. The dealer declares where the ball landed in the pocket (e.g., "Red 9").
  7. The dealer collects the chips that missed and pays out for the bets that hit the target.

The chips are not regular chips, but color-coded chips for each player, which are used exclusively in roulette.
When placing a bet, you can either place the chips on the table yourself or declare it to the dealer and hand over the chips.
Unclear bets or bets placed after the deadline will be forfeited as fouls.
The details differ from house to house.
In many cases, bets can be placed before or after the ball is thrown in, but in some casinos, such as those in Asian regions, bets can only be placed before the ball is thrown in. Since this rule greatly disadvantages the customer, a mechanical wheel is sometimes used where the ball is thrown in by a machine rather than a dealer.
Many casinos have boards that display a history of the numbers that have been played, which can be referred to if necessary.

Types of bets

Roulette bets range from betting twice the payout on red or black, odd or even numbers, to betting 36 times the payout on the only certain number coming up, called a one-card bet.
0 (including 00 and 000) is green and is not considered even or small, and the dealer's total is taken against outside bets.

inside bed

  • One-eye bet - bet on one specific number.
    The dividend is 36 times.
  • Eye betting - betting on two adjacent numbers.
    The dividend is 18 times.
  • 3-eye betting - betting on three numbers in a horizontal row.
    The dividend is 12 times.
  • Four-eyed betting - betting on four numbers in the four directions of the cross or 0, 1, 2, 3 (European style only).
    The dividend is 9x.
  • 5-Eye Wager - In American style, betting on two horizontal rows of five numbers (0, 00, 1, 2, 3). The payout is 7x. It is the only betting method with different (unfavorable) odds, and some houses offer the same payout as the 6-eyes bet.
    Many houses do not allow this method of betting.
  • Six-eye wager - bet on six numbers in two horizontal rows.
    The dividend is 6x.

outside bed

  • Vertical Row - Bet on 12 numbers in one vertical row. The payout is 3x.
  • Large, medium, or small - bet on 1-12, 13-24, or 25-3.
    Dividends are tripled.
  • First and second half - bet on either 1-18 or 19-36.
    Dividends are doubled.
  • Odd/Even - bet on either odd or even numbers (except 0 and 00).
    Dividends are doubled.
  • Red/Black - Bet on either red or black numbers.
    Dividends are doubled.

Other

Europe offers other types of bets than those listed above.
The bet is communicated to the dealer by vocalization.

  • Finale - bet against the first place of a number.
    The payout is the same as a 3-eye or 4-eye wager.
  • Around Zero - bet on the number near zero in the wheel sequence.
    There are two patterns: one is to bet on 17 peripheral pieces and the other is to bet on 7 peripheral pieces.
